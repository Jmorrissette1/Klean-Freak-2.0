About Us

Welcome to Klean Freak Car Wash, where cleanliness meets automotive excellence! At Klean Freak, we are committed to providing an unparalleled car washing experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Our passion for perfection and dedication to customer satisfaction set us apart as the premier destination for all your vehicle cleaning needs.
Klean Freak Car Wash is owned and operated locally by Car Guys who have had a lifelong dream of opening a car wash. With that dream finally coming true and their first two locations opening in Kitsap County – one in Silverdale and one in Poulsbo. We are so excited to be bringing “A Feel Good Klean” to your neighborhood.
At the heart of Klean Freak is our state-of-the-art facility equipped with the latest in car wash technology. We have cutting-edge systems and eco-friendly products to ensure your vehicle not only looks fantastic but also receives the utmost care. Our advanced cleaning techniques are designed to protect your car’s finish while removing dirt, grime, and environmental contaminants.
What truly sets Klean Freak apart is our team of meticulous professionals who share a genuine love for automobiles. With their expertise, your vehicle will be treated with the utmost care and attention to detail. Whether it’s a compact car, a rugged SUV, or a luxury sedan, we tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each vehicle, leaving no spot untouched.
We understand that your time is valuable, and we strive to provide a swift and efficient car wash experience without compromising on quality. Our commitment to excellence extends to our customer service, where friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist you with any inquiries or special requests.
At Klean Freak, we take pride in our eco-conscious approach, utilizing water-saving technologies and biodegradable cleaning agents to minimize our environmental impact. We believe in contributing to a cleaner and greener planet while maintaining the high standards that our customers expect.
Whether you’re a car enthusiast seeking the perfect shine or a busy professional in need of a quick and reliable car wash, Klean Freak is here to exceed your expectations. Experience the next level of car care – visit Klean Freak Car Wash and let your vehicle shine with the brilliance it deserves!
